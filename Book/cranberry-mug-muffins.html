Cranberry Mug Muffins

Ingredients
  • 1 cup Processed Oats
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Canola Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Handful of cranberries
Directions
  1. Mix ingredients in bowl. Put in ziplock bag. Store until ready to use.
  2. Mix 3 parts mix with 1 part milk and microwave from 1:30-2:30 mins.
