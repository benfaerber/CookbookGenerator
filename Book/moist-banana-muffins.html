Moist Banana Muffins

Ingredients
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup white sugar

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup mayonnaise

Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
  2. Stir the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and sugar together in a bowl. Add the bananas, mayonnaise; stir until well combined. Pour into prepared muffin cups to about 3/4 full.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and the tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 20 minutes.
